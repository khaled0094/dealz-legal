Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 6, 2026

Dealz ("we", "us", "our") is a marketplace app operated by Dealz Marketplace. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information.

Information We Collect

  • Account data: name, email, and phone number.
  • Listings: title, description, price, city, category, and photos you upload.
  • Messages: chat messages sent between users.
  • Usage data: basic app activity needed to provide the service.

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To publish listings and enable buyer-seller communication.
  • To provide support and respond to requests.
  • To keep the marketplace safe (fraud prevention, moderation, and blocking/reporting).

How We Share Information

  • With other users: your listings and chat messages are visible to the people you contact.
  • With service providers: we use third-party infrastructure (such as Firebase) to store data and deliver the service.
  • For legal reasons if required by law.

Data Storage and Security

We store data using third-party cloud services. We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no system is 100% secure.

Data Retention

We keep your data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and data by contacting us.

Your Choices

  • You can edit or delete your listings.
  • You can request account deletion by contacting support.

Children's Privacy

Dealz is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

International Users

If you access the app from outside Sweden, your data may be processed in other countries where our service providers operate.
