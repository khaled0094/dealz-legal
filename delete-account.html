Delete Account - Dealz

If you want to delete your Dealz account and associated data, use one of the methods below.

Method 1 (in app)

  1. Open Dealz
  2. Go to Profile
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm deletion

Method 2 (email request)

Send your request from your registered email to: support.dealz@dealzmarketplace.com

What is deleted

  • Account profile data
  • Your listings and listing metadata
  • Associated app data linked to your account

What may be retained temporarily

  • Security, fraud-prevention, and legal compliance records
  • Backups for a limited retention period (up to 30 days)
